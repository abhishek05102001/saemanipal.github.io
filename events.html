KRAFTWAGEN(EXTERNAL)-Techtatva

Date:May 05, 2021

Kraftwagen is an outdoor event at Techtatva and its participants would have to build their own bot which would be tested on a variety of hard terrains ranging from hanging bridges ,uneven rocks and even underwater.

This event stretches to a span of 2days. Day1 begins at the automobile workshop where participants build their own bot by shaping, cutting and smoothing the edges of a chasis from an aluminium sheet and several motors, wires are connected to this chasis to build a ground breaking bot capable of multi-terrain locomotion.

Day 2 brings these bots to live ,where all participants control their bot on the harsh terrains in a time bound environment. The participant with the fastest timing wins the grand total from a pool of 12000. The whole experience is electric and also serves an educational purpose to its participants by providing hands on experience.

AUTOWEEK(EXTERNAL)

Date:May 05, 2021

Autoweek is an event organized by SAE-IM to learn and gain experience on every component of an automobile including Engine, Transmission, Chassis, Suspension, Brakes, Tires; just name it.

The event consists of 2 parts- theory sessions, where you learn about every component and lab sessions, where you see every component learnt physically in the engines and automobile labs.

The aim of the event is for the participants to have an upper hand in the knowledge of automobiles and that too comprising both, of theoretical and practical knowledge.

IC ENGINES WORKSHOP(INTERNAL)

Date:May 05, 2021

This workshop aims at educating us about the basic functionality, working of an internal combustion engine. It provides in depth knowledge of various aspects of engines like the difference between a petrol and diesel engine, engines used in heavy vehicles. This workshop further throws light and provides in depth explanation of the various components of an IC engine like the spark plug, crank shaft, flywheel,etc . This workshop also talks about maintaining, oiling and servicing the engines of our cars for a smooth hassle free travel. Finally aspects like formulas related to IC engines.

UNSCREW ENGINE (EXTERNAL)

Date:May 05, 2021

The unscrew engine workshop is one of our flagship events.
This is the chance to awaken your inner engine hog, as we give you a chance to tend to the heart of a car. As the name itself suggests we literally let you disassemble the engine taking out every part, be it the cam shafts, cylinder, piston and its rings, fuel injector to its bare minimum for better understanding of every part that there is. You won't leave the workshop with any doubts regarding an engine after this!

JET ENGINES WORKSHOP(EXTERNAL)

Date:May 05, 2021

The jet engines workshop focuses on building a technical understanding of the various types of jet engines and the distinct differences between them by giving its participants a glimpse into the intriguing functioning of jet engine and a wind tunnel. The event stretches to a span of 2 days. Day 1 is theoretical and various concepts such as lift, drag, wing concepts and a lot more exciting concepts were covered. Further dissection of a jet engine took place in cross section which enabled to view the various parts and functions of different parts. Day 2 begins in the propulsion lab where participants visualised the aeronautical designs from the previous day and ends with the demonstration of a functioning water tunnel used to run aerodynamic tests.

ENGINES WORKSHOP(Online)

Date:December 19, 2021

The workshop conducted by the managing committee (ManComm) of SAE-IM last November was aimed at introducing our juniors to the marvelous world of engines. Several topics from types of engines and parts of an engine to turbochargers and superchargers were explained in great detail during the course of this workshop. The workshop not only helped the juniors with expanding their knowledge on engines, but it also helped the ManComm enhance their knowledge on several automotive concepts.
