About Us

Welcome to the official site of SAE Manipal. SAE stands for Society of Automobile Engineers. SAEINDIA is a Indian nonprofit engineering and scientific society dedicated to the advancement of the mobility industry in India.

MISSION

The mission of SAE-India is to create a non-profit engineering and scientific society dedicating itself to the advancement of mobility industry in India.
SAE-IM is club which operates under SAE-India which works towards achieving advance mobility knowledge and solutions in the automotive industry for students and to create an open platform for petrolheads to express their thoughts and ideas. SAE organises several workshops and competetions with a goal of uniting and tutoring tech enthusiasts.

We at SAE-IM (Society of Automotive Engineers Manipal), a team of dedicated and professional gearheads, aim towards adding to the automobile community, in both knowledge and numbers by introducing the students of Manipal to the Automobile world through our extensive and all year round informative seminars, hands-on workshops and vibrant events along with a convivial web of professors and students to help give rise to a passionate and creative generation of students in the world of mobility.

VISION

The vision of SAE-IM is to strive for innovation and excellence in Automotive Design and Concepts. We strive towards creating an extended family of automotive enthusiasts and indulging into the tank of knowledge together. SAE-IM believes in utilizing hands-on experience rather than traditional class room interaction to educate its members for a holistic educational experience.

We, at SAE (Society of Automotive Engineers), being a vast international organization of over 128,000 engineers and technical experts, are dedicated to bringing about a global advancement in the field of mobility engineering by helping bridge together a substantial web of students, engineers and companies; attracting, managing and distributing automobile related information through education and technical publications & forming beneficial affiliations and interfaces to help encourage innovation and add value to the field of Automotive Engineering.
